Card Game
Euchre

Euchre (pronounced “YOO-kur”) is a trick-taking card game popular in Michigan. It is most commonly played by four people in two partnerships with a deck of 24 cards. Partnerships accumulate points for winning tricks, and the game continues until one side reaches the maximum number of points. This project provides a simulation of the game. It allows up-to four players to play. Any amount of the players can be actual users, or they can also be bots.
